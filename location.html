Parking

  • There is pay & display street parking available on some of the side roads.
    Parking is free after 6:30pm, however on match days (when ARSENAL F.C is playing at the Emerites stadium) parking restrictions do not end until 8:30pm.
  • There is also red route parking bays that allow for 1 hour max FREE parking, these bays are not effected by match days.
    1-Hour Stay: If a bay permits a 1-hour stay, you must follow it, and you cannot return to the same space for a further 2 hours (no return within 2 hours).
  • Alternatively, there's Morrisons car park round the back of Holloway road that offers FREE parkign for 2 hours.
